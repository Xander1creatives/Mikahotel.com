About Our Hotels

A Legacy of Luxury Hospitality

Mika Convention Center is located about 10KM from the Kenneth Kaunda international Airport in the outskates of Chongwe district that boarders Lusaka city.

Mika Hotel Kabulonga is Located in the heart of Lusaka’s Kabulonga residential area, Mika Hotel is 25Km from KK international airport and about 10km from the central business district

Mika Hotel Jesmondine Situated in the residential area of Jesmondine on the corner of First and Central Street. Mika Lodge is about 10Km away from the Central Business district and 15KM from the Kenneth Kaunda International Airport.

Chamba-valley Exotic Located in the quiet and peaceful suburbs of Chamba valley away from the hustle and bustle of city life. It offers Four-star accommodation with its 78 modern fully furnished comfortable rooms.
