About MIKA Hotels

MIKA Hotels offers an unparalleled luxury experience in the heart of the city. With over 20 years of excellence in hospitality, we pride ourselves on providing world-class amenities and exceptional service to all our guests.

Our hotel features 150 elegantly designed rooms, a state-of-the-art fitness center, multiple dining options, and versatile event spaces perfect for both business and leisure travelers.

At MIKA Hotels, we believe in creating memorable experiences that exceed our guests' expectations. Our dedicated team is committed to ensuring your stay is comfortable, enjoyable, and truly unforgettable.
